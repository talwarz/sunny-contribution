We are collecting support for Sunny Matta's family.

Sound & Light Industry ki taraf se hum Sunny Matta ji aur unke parivaar ke liye support fund collect kar rahe hain.

Sunny Matta ji hamari industry ke ek bahut hi talented, dedicated aur helpful insaan rahe hain. Unhone pichle 20+ saalon se Sound & Light Industry ko apni seva aur expertise di hai. Apne career ke dauran unhone na sirf countless shows aur projects mein yogdaan diya, balki kai naye professionals ko train aur guide bhi kiya.

Aaj industry mein kaam kar rahe bahut se successful Lighting Designers aur Technicians unhi ke guidance aur training ka natija hain. Unka yogdaan aur dusron ki madad karne ka jazba hamesha yaad rakha jayega.

Aaiye, hum sab milkar Sunny Matta ji aur unke parivaar ke saath khade hon aur apni taraf se jitna sambhav ho sake support pradan karein. Har chhota ya bada contribution unke parivaar ke liye bahut maayne rakhta hai. 🙏🏻

"Together, let's support a person who has spent his life supporting and uplifting others in our industry." ❤️
